# Product Pricing Tiers

## Overview
We offer three pricing tiers designed to land-and-expand across company sizes.

## Tier 1: Starter — $499/month
- Up to 5 users
- Core Platform only
- Standard dashboards
- Email support (48h SLA)
- CSV data import
- Ideal for: SMB, early-stage companies

## Tier 2: Growth — $1,999/month
- Up to 25 users
- Core Platform + Analytics Add-on
- Custom dashboards
- Slack + email alerts
- CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Priority support (8h SLA)
- Ideal for: Mid-market, Series A-B companies

## Tier 3: Enterprise — Custom Pricing ($5,000–$50,000/month)
- Unlimited users
- Full product suite (all modules)
- API access + webhooks
- SSO / SAML
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Custom onboarding + training
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Ideal for: Enterprise, 1,000+ employees

- Volume seats (100+): Custom negotiation, max 35% off list
- **Never discount below 50% without VP Sales approval**

# Sales Qualification Rules

## MEDDIC Framework (Required for all $25K+ deals)

| Component | Required Questions | Disqualify If... |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **M**etrics | What KPIs will improve? By how much? | No measurable outcome defined |
| **E**conomic Buyer | Who signs the contract? | No access to EB after 2 attempts |
| **D**ecision Criteria | What does a successful vendor look like? | We don't meet 2+ criteria |
| **D**ecision Process | How is vendor selected? What's the timeline? | Timeline > 6 months with no urgency |
| **I**dentify Pain | What happens if nothing changes? | No compelling event |
| **C**hampion | Who is internally selling for us? | No internal champion identified |

## Deal Stage Definitions & Exit Criteria

| Stage | Definition | Exit Criteria |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| Prospecting | Account identified, no contact | First meeting booked |
| Discovery | Pain and fit confirmed | MEDDIC partially complete, demo scheduled |
| Demo | Product demo delivered | Positive eval, proposal requested |
| Proposal | Pricing sent | Verbal commitment, legal review started |
| Negotiation | Legal/commercial review | Signed contract |
| Closed Won | Contract signed | Revenue recognized |
| Closed Lost | Deal lost | Loss reason logged in CRM |

# Expansion Strategy

## Expand in Place Framework

### Trigger 1: Seat Expansion
**Signal**: Active users ≥ 75% of licensed seats for 2 consecutive months
**Action**: CSM schedules expansion call; AE prepares seat upgrade quote
**Target**: Upsell 25–50% additional seats

### Trigger 2: Module Cross-Sell
**Signal**: 90+ days on Core Platform, usage events > 5,000/month
**Action**: AE introduces relevant add-on module (Analytics, API, Compliance)
**Target**: 1 additional module per account within 12 months

### Trigger 3: Tier Upgrade
**Signal**: Customer consistently at Tier limit (users, API calls, integrations)
**Action**: CSM + AE joint call with ROI analysis
**Target**: Move 20% of Growth tier accounts to Enterprise annually

## NRR Calculation
```
NRR = (Starting ARR + Expansion - Churn - Contraction) / Starting ARR
Target NRR: ≥ 120%
```

## Key Expansion Metrics
- **Time to first expansion**: Target < 12 months from initial close
- **Expansion ARR as % of total ARR growth**: Target 40%
- **Accounts with 2+ products**: Target 35% of base
