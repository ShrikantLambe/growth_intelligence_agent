# Growth Strategy Playbook

## Company Growth Philosophy
We operate as a product-led growth (PLG) company with a sales-assisted motion for enterprise accounts. Our growth strategy balances new logo acquisition with aggressive net revenue retention.

## Annual Growth Targets

| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| New ARR | $8M | Track monthly |
| Net Revenue Retention | ≥ 120% | Review quarterly |
| Pipeline Coverage | 3.5x quota | Review weekly |
| Win Rate | ≥ 28% | Track by segment |
| Payback Period | < 18 months | Track annually |

## Growth Pillars

### 1. New Logo Acquisition
- **Primary motion**: Outbound SDR + inbound from content/SEO
- **Target segments**: FinTech (highest ACV) and HRTech (fastest sales cycle)
- **Key channels**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Referral program, G2/Capterra reviews
- **Quota per AE**: $1.5M new ARR annually

### 2. Expansion Revenue
Expansion is our highest-priority growth lever. A 10% improvement in NRR has more impact than equivalent new logo growth.

- **Triggers for expansion conversation**:
  - Active users > 80% of licensed seats (seat expansion)
  - Customer using 2+ products (cross-sell opportunity)
  - Usage events > 10,000/month (power user segment)
  - QBR within 60 days of renewal

- **Expansion playbooks**:
  - Seat expansion: CSM-led, triggered at 75% seat utilization
  - Module cross-sell: AE-led after 90 days of Core Platform adoption
  - Enterprise upgrade: triggered by compliance or SSO requests

### 3. Retention & Risk Management

**Churn Indicators (Red Flags)**:
- No login activity in the last 21 days
- Active users declined >30% month-over-month
- Support ticket volume increased >50% MoM
- Champion departure (tracked via LinkedIn/CRM)
- Contract renewal within 90 days + low health score

**Intervention Playbooks**:
- **Red accounts**: Executive sponsor call within 5 business days
- **Yellow accounts**: CSM check-in + tailored success plan
- **Upcoming renewals**: QBR 60 days prior to renewal date

### 4. Pipeline Generation

Monthly pipeline targets by source:
- Inbound: 40% of pipeline
- Outbound: 35% of pipeline
- Partner/referral: 15% of pipeline
- Events: 10% of pipeline

## Segmentation Strategy

| Segment | ACV Range | Sales Motion | Primary Owner |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| SMB | < $15K | Self-serve + PLG | Marketing |
| Mid-Market | $15K–$75K | Sales-assisted | AE + CSM |
| Enterprise | > $75K | Enterprise sales | Sr. AE + SE + CSM |

## Quarterly Planning Cadence
- **Monthly**: Pipeline review, win/loss analysis, churn risk review
- **Quarterly**: ICP refinement, channel performance, NRR deep dive
- **Annually**: Market sizing, pricing review, expansion market assessment
